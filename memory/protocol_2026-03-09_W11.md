## Weekly Schedule

| Day | Type | Intensity | Workout | Duration |
|-----|------|-----------|---------|----------|
| **Monday** | Swim | Easy (80%) | 1200m easy freestyle: 200m warm-up + 8x100m easy (rest 20s) + 200m cool-down. Focus on breathing rhythm and form. | 35 min |
| **Tuesday** | Bike + Strength | Hard (20%) | **Bike**: 10min warm-up + 5x4min hard intervals (threshold, 85-90% HR) at 90-95 RPM + 10min cool-down (50 min total). **Then**: Strength A (see below) | 50 min bike + 35 min strength |
| **Wednesday** | Run | Easy (80%) | 4 miles easy run. Conversational pace, HR <150. Focus on cadence and form. | 40 min |
| **Thursday** | Run + Strength | Hard (20%) | **Run**: 10min easy warm-up + 5x1000m hard (90%+ max HR, ~4:40 pace) with 3min easy jog recovery + 10min easy cool-down. **Then**: Strength B (see below) | 55 min run + 35 min strength |
| **Friday** | Swim | Easy (80%) | 1000m easy swim: 200m warm-up + 6x100m freestyle + 4x50m kick + 200m cool-down. Mix of drills and steady pace. | 30 min |
| **Saturday** | Brick (Long) | Hard (20%) | **Bike**: 70min ride (20min easy warm-up + 40min at 80-85 RPM steady pace + 10min cool-down). **Run**: Immediately transition to 20min easy run off-bike to practice transitions. | 90 min total |
| **Sunday** | Active Recovery | Easy (80%) | 30min easy walk or very easy swim. Focus on moving, not training. Stretching and foam rolling (priority: quad recovery). | 30 min |
